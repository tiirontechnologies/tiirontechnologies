# About Us:
Tiiron Technologies is a technology-driven company focused on transforming education and institutions through Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, intelligent software, and scalable digital platforms.

We design and build modern learning ecosystems, enterprise software, and automation solutions that help institutions operate smarter, teach better, and innovate faster.


# Products & Services

### Products

* **Tiiron LMS** — Learning infrastructure for modern institutions
* **Tiiron ERP** — Unified system for academic and operational management
* **Job Portal** — Structured hiring platform connecting talent and companies
* **AI Interviewer** — Intelligent system for automated candidate evaluation

### Services

* **Inacademic** — Bridging the gap between college and industry through real-world experience
* **Tiiron Academy** — Early-stage programs in AI, coding, and emerging technologies for school students
